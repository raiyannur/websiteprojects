What gets extracted
The backend reads each semester block in the PDF and extracts the GPA line for each semester. Courses with W (withdrawn) or Z grades are skipped. CGPA is calculated from the cumulative grade points and credits on the final page.

Supported format: NSU-style transcripts (and similar layouts with "GPA :" rows per semester block). Other university formats may need manual CSV entry.
